Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your Privacy Matters

At Cypher Core, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This page outlines our privacy practices and policies.

Data Collection

Cypher Core processes all encryption and encoding operations directly in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit your input data to any server.

Client-Side Processing

All cipher transformations happen locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your text never leaves your device during the encryption/encoding process.

Analytics (If Applicable)

We may use anonymous analytics to understand usage patterns, but we never track or store your actual cipher content.

Contact

If you have questions about our privacy policy, please contact us.
